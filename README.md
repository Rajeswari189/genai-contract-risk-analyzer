VENDOR SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Vendor Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on 1st February 2026 between:

ABC Retail Pvt Ltd, Hyderabad ("Client")
AND
TechNova Solutions, Bengaluru ("Vendor")

1. Scope of Services
The Vendor shall provide software maintenance and IT support services to the Client.

2. Payment Terms
The Client agrees to pay INR 5,00,000 annually.
Payment must be made within 15 days of invoice.
Late payment will attract 2% monthly interest.

3. Term
This Agreement shall remain valid for 3 years from the effective date.
The contract will auto-renew for another 1 year unless terminated in writing 60 days before expiry.

4. Termination
The Client may terminate the agreement at any time with 30 days notice.
The Vendor may terminate only in case of non-payment exceeding 60 days.

5. Indemnity
The Vendor agrees to indemnify and hold harmless the Client against any third-party claims arising from negligence.

6. Limitation of Liability
The Vendor's liability shall not exceed the total fees paid under this Agreement.

7. Intellectual Property
All work products developed under this Agreement shall be the exclusive property of the Client.

8. Non-Compete
The Vendor shall not provide similar services to direct competitors of the Client within Hyderabad for 2 years.

9. Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of India.
Jurisdiction shall be courts of Hyderabad only.
